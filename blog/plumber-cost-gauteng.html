COC Plumbing Certificate Cost in South Africa

A Certificate of Compliance (COC) is legally required in South Africa when:

  • Selling a property
  • Making an insurance claim for water damage
  • After a new plumbing installation
  • Connecting to a municipal water supply

The cost of a COC certificate from a PIRB-registered plumber in Gauteng typically ranges from R800 to R2,500 for a standard residential inspection and certificate. When the COC is issued as part of a new installation (e.g., geyser), the cost is usually included in the job quote.

Is it cheaper to hire a plumber without PIRB registration?
